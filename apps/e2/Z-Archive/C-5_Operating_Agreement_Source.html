Operating Agreement for Targon LLC

This Operating Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of August 15, 2025, by and between Jarrar Amjad, the sole member (the "Member"), and Nezha Elalbani, the manager (the "Manager"), for Targon LLC, a Kansas limited liability company (the "Company").

1. Formation and Purpose

  • The Company is organized under the laws of the State of Kansas as a manager-managed limited liability company.
  • The purpose of the Company is to engage in private label retail, including any lawful activities related thereto.

2. Management Structure

  • The Company is manager-managed. Nezha Elalbani is hereby appointed as the Manager of the Company, replacing all prior managers.
  • Jarrar Amjad, as the Member, retains ultimate authority over the Company but delegates day-to-day management to the Manager.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Manager's Duties: Nezha Elalbani, as the Manager, shall be responsible for all administrative and operational activities for Targon LLC until the LLC is changed to a member-managed structure, including:
    • Signing documents on behalf of the Company.
    • Managing the Company's bank accounts (e.g., deposits, withdrawals).
    • Reviewing and signing warehouse lease agreements or similar contracts.
  • Member's Authority: reserves the right to:
    • Approve major decisions, such as entering into contracts exceeding $20,000, borrowing money, or selling Company assets.
    • Remove or replace the Manager at any time.

4. Compensation

  • Nezha Elalbani shall be compensated as a manager at a rate of $4,000 per month for her services.
  • Payments shall be made on the 1st day of each month.

5. Manager Status

  • Nezha Elalbani serves as the Manager of the Company.
  • The Company will handle all appropriate tax withholdings and reporting for the Manager's compensation.

6. Tax Treatment

  • As a single-member LLC, the Company is treated as a disregarded entity for federal tax purposes unless Jarrar Amjad elects otherwise.
  • Jarrar Amjad is responsible for reporting all Company income and expenses on his personal tax return.

7. Duration and Termination

  • This Agreement remains in effect until the Company transitions to a member-managed structure or is dissolved.
  • The member may terminate Manager(s) with a 30 days' written notice.

8. Amendments

  • This Agreement may only be amended by a written document signed by Jarrar Amjad.

9. Governing Law

  • This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Kansas.

10. Dispute Resolution

  • Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through mediation in Manhattan, Kansas, or, if necessary, through binding arbitration under Kansas law.
