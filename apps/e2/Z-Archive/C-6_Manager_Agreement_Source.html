Manager Agreement

This Manager Agreement (the "Agreement") is made effective as of August 15, 2025, between Targon LLC, a Kansas limited liability company owned by Jarrar Amjad (the "Company"), and Nezha Elalbani, an individual residing at 5300 Glenside Dr #2007, Henrico, Virginia 23228 (the "Manager").

1. Services

The Manager agrees to provide administrative and operational support services (the "Services") as the Manager of Targon LLC until the Company transitions to a member-managed structure. These Services include:

  • Signing documents on behalf of the Company.
  • Managing the Company's bank accounts, including deposits and withdrawals.
  • Reviewing and signing warehouse lease agreements or similar contracts.
  • Managing day-to-day U.S. logistics, inventory control at the 3PL/warehouse, and overseeing order fulfillment.

2. Term

This Agreement begins on August 15, 2025, and continues until the Company transitions to a member-managed structure or is terminated earlier by either party with 30 days' written notice.

3. Compensation

The Company shall pay the Manager a fixed fee of $4,000 per month for the Services, payable on the 1st day of each month via bank transfer or check. No additional expenses or reimbursements will be provided unless agreed in writing.

4. Manager Status

The Manager serves as the operational manager of the Company. The Manager has full control over the manner and means of performing the Services, subject to the Company's strategic direction. The Company will handle all appropriate employment tax withholdings and reporting as required by law.

5. Confidentiality

The Manager agrees to keep all Company information confidential and will not disclose it to third parties without the Company's written consent, except as required by law.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice. Upon termination, the Manager will be paid for Services completed up to the termination date.

7. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Kansas.

8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes any prior agreements related to the Services.
